2. DEVICE PERMISSIONS

To provide the core functionality of our arithmetic challenges and the integrated alarm clock feature, the Application requests specific device permissions. These permissions are used strictly locally on your device and are not used to collect, store, or transmit any personal data:

  • Alarms & Background Execution: Permissions such as SET_ALARM and RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED allow the app to schedule and trigger your math challenge alarms accurately, even after your device is restarted.
  • Media & Power Management: Permissions including FOREGROUND_SERVICE, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK, and WAKE_LOCK are required to reliably play the alarm sound in the background when it is time for a challenge.
  • Screen & Interface Controls: The USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT permission is used to display the ringing alarm and math problem prominently on your screen, ensuring you do not miss your scheduled wake-up or challenge.
  • Shortcuts: The INSTALL_SHORTCUT permission allows you to place quick-access icons on your home screen.
